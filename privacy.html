Privacy policy

Last updated June 2026.

Lynch-the-dog ("we", "us") operates this website. This policy explains how we collect and use your information in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data we collect

When you submit the contact form we collect your first name, email address, and any message you provide. We use this solely to send you one dog photo and, if you consent, occasional emails about Lynch.

Cookies

We group cookies into three categories:

  • Strictly necessary — always on. Used only to remember your privacy choice (stored locally in your browser). No tracking.
  • Analytics — off by default. With your consent we use Google Analytics (loaded via Google Tag Manager) to understand which pages people enjoy. We may also use Mixpanel for product analytics.
  • Marketing — off by default. With your consent we may measure campaign performance and show Lynch on other sites.

Analytics and marketing cookies are loaded with Google Consent Mode v2: until you opt in, no identifiers or personal data are sent. If you opt in and later change your mind, any analytics cookies already set in your browser are cleared automatically. We will ask again after 6 months, or sooner if our cookie policy materially changes. You can change your choice at any time via the Cookie settings link.

Your rights (GDPR)

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We keep your email for as long as you remain subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

Contact

Questions? Reach us via the contact page.
